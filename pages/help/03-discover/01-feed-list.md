## Feed

The [Feed](/feed) shows creations from people you follow, in order. Use it to keep up with work you care about.

To add more to your feed, [follow creators](/help/connect/follow-creators) you like. For discovery beyond who you follow, see [Explore list](/help/discover/explore-list).
