## Feature requests

We want to hear what would make parascene better for you. Feature requests and feedback help shape the product.

- Use in-app feedback or contact options when available.
- For bugs or issues, include what you were doing and what you expected so we can reproduce and fix.

We review suggestions regularly and prioritize based on impact and community input.
